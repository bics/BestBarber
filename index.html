Best Barber

Welcome to Best Barber, where style meets tradition. Established in 1959 by two friends, Cesarina Polyphonte and Patrícia Odelia, with a shared passion for customer service and traditional values, we’ve been serving the community with pride ever since. In our humble establishment, we provide exceptional service for everyone, from the youth to the elders. No matter the task, we aim to deliver perfection with our skilled hands and attention to detail. Whether you're here for a classic cut, a modern fade, or a relaxing shave, our goal is simple: to make you look and feel your best. At Best Barber, you're not just a client—you're part of our family.

The veteran of the shop, Cesarina’s hands have shaped the hair of countless clients over the years. With over 30 years of experience, she’s a master of classic cuts and expert fades, blending timeless style with modern flair. Known for her warm personality and eye for detail, Cesarina’s been perfecting her craft since the very beginning. When she’s not behind the chair, she loves gardening, creating beautiful floral arrangements, and spending time with her family.

Co-founder and a true innovator, Patrícia is all about pushing boundaries while keeping the essence of tradition. With her love for both contemporary styles and old-school techniques, she’s the go-to for creative designs and fresh trends. Patrícia’s attention to detail is unmatched, and her ability to bring out the best in every client is what keeps them coming back. Outside of the shop, she’s an avid reader, enjoys hiking in nature, and is a regular at local art galleries, always seeking inspiration.
